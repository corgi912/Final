Types of Pet

Many people own many different types of pets.

Dogs

A type very popular pet are dogs. People own a dog as a pet for many reasons. They can keep people company and can teach people responsibility and empathy.

Cats

Cats are also a favorite of pet among the world. Cats are low maintenence, keeps your house rodent-free,and just petting the cat can help reduce stress.

Hamster

Hamsters are pretty low-maintenence and are easier to take care of compared to other pets. They are inexpensive to feed and do not require special grooming needs.
